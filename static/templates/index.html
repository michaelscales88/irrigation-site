Introduction

Water conservation efforts are essential with the continuous environmental and technological changes happening today. The increasing population and excessive energy consumption have made finding immediate solutions vital to preserving the precious resource, water. Texas State University and SENSCO are committed to resolving this global issue. SENSCO is a startup devoted to leaving the world better than we found it by tackling important social issues such as water conservation. Our Smart Irrigation System will implement wireless sensors that transmit data to the cloud to be collected and analyzed. The cloud analysis will determine a watering schedule for the environment being monitored. The goal of this project is to demonstrate a minimally intrusive design that can be introduced to residential and commercial irrigation systems
